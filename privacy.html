🔒 Privacy & Disclaimer

Your privacy and safety

This is a school project website. Please read the information below to understand how it works and what it is — and is not — designed to do.

🎓
Educational purpose only
This website is a 12 Enterprise Computing school project created for educational awareness purposes only. All content has been developed to raise awareness about student stress, burnout and wellbeing and is not intended to represent official health, psychological or medical advice of any kind. The information presented throughout this website should not be used as a substitute for professional support or guidance.
🔐
No data is stored
Any answers provided in the stress calculator quiz are used only to calculate an on-screen result during your current session. No information is saved to a server, database or external system. No accounts are created, no personal information is collected and no tracking or analytics tools are used on this website. When you close or leave the page your quiz answers are permanently gone.
🏥
Not a medical tool
This website does not provide medical, psychological or clinical diagnoses of any kind. The stress calculator quiz and wellbeing results are illustrative and educational only — they are based on general self-reflection questions and are not a clinically validated assessment tool. If you are experiencing serious mental health concerns, stress or anxiety, please speak with a trusted adult, school counsellor, general practitioner or qualified mental health professional.

📊 About the statistics

All statistics and research data presented on the Statistics page have been sourced from published Australian and international research studies and wellbeing organisations including ReachOut Australia, the Black Dog Institute, the Sleep Health Foundation, the Australian Communications and Media Authority and UNSW Sydney. While every effort has been made to present this data accurately, the figures used are based on published research findings and may not reflect the exact experience of every individual student.

💡 About the tips

The wellbeing and productivity tips presented on the Tips page are based on general evidence-based strategies sourced from reputable health and education organisations. These tips are intended as general lifestyle suggestions only. Individual circumstances vary and what works for one person may not work for another. If you are struggling with your mental health, please seek personalised support from a qualified professional rather than relying solely on the general advice provided here.

💚 Need support?

You are not alone.

If you or someone you know is struggling with stress, anxiety or mental health, please reach out. Help is available — and asking for support is a sign of strength, not weakness.
